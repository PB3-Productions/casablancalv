About This Space

A Private Las Vegas Estate Built for Unforgettable Stays

Step inside a private Las Vegas estate built for unforgettable stays. This spacious 7-bedroom retreat blends the feel of a boutique resort with the privacy of an entire home. The layout is ideal for groups who want to stay together without feeling crowded, with multiple gathering areas, comfortable sleeping arrangements, game spaces, outdoor dining, and a resort-style backyard.

Spend the day by the pool, unwind in the hot tub, cook for the group in one of the kitchen areas, enjoy poker, pool, shuffleboard, Xbox, cornhole, or oversized games, then settle in for a relaxed night just minutes from Las Vegas entertainment.

The home offers the best of both worlds: close access to the Strip, airport, dining, nightlife, and attractions — with a private estate setting where your group can recharge between adventures.

Guest Access & Location

Prime Las Vegas Location

Guests have access to the entire home, front yard, backyard, and driveway, excluding locked owner/cleaning supply closets. Entry is handled through a digital keypad for easy group access.

Stay close to the best of Las Vegas while enjoying the privacy of a spacious estate setting. The property is positioned near the Strip, airport, UNLV area, casinos, dining, nightlife, and entertainment.

Nearby: Las Vegas Strip · Harry Reid International Airport · MGM Grand · Bellagio · Fremont Street · Las Vegas Convention Center · UNLV / Thomas & Mack Center

A vehicle or rideshare is recommended. The home includes onsite/private parking, and guests can easily use Uber, Lyft, taxis, or private transportation.
